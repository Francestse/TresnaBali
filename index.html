Welcome to Tresna Bali Cooking School

We're crazy, culinary cooks at heart.

Why Tresna Bali?

With us, you won’t just see the place we love, you’ll barrel into it – into our traditions, our riverside garden, our kitchens and our Balinese way of life – taking your own special place around our welcoming table.

Sure, you’ll learn our coveted recipes and fill your stomach to beyond satiation, but you’ll go deeper: learning about local Balinese culture, pick your own organic ingredients and get to the heart of cooking, experiencing each intriguing element of everything you’ll put in your mouth.

Our Cooking Classes

  • Farm-to-table menu of Authentic Balinese Dishes
  • Feast on Organic Ingredients you'll pick yourself
  • Individual Modern Cooking Stations
  • Intimate Hands-On Classes (Maximum of 10 Cooks)
  • Riverside Organic Garden with Traditional Outdoor Kitchen
  • Complimentary transfers from Central Ubud (7 minutes away)
