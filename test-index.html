Welcome to Tresna Bali Cooking School

We're crazy, culinary cooks at heart.

Why Tresna Bali?

With us, you won’t just see the place we love, you’ll barrel into it – into our traditions, our riverside garden, our kitchens and our Balinese way of life – taking your own special place around our welcoming table.

Sure, you’ll learn our coveted recipes and fill your stomach to beyond satiation, but you’ll go deeper: learning about local Balinese culture, pick your own organic ingredients and get to the heart of cooking, experiencing each intriguing element of everything you’ll put in your mouth.

Our Cooking Classes

  • Farm-to-table menu of Authentic Balinese Dishes

  • Individual Modern Cooking Stations

Preparing an Authentic Balinese Feast at Tresna Bali Cooking School

Unsponsored Blog by Passport To Wellness, Brittney Ayers

In early March, Brittney booked for a May class. We accommodated a date change to August since she informed us months in advance. Meanwhile, participants were telling us that they preferred a smaller class size and were willing to pay more. In June, we tested a new class format - from 12 participants to 6 - essentially doubling the price for each participant. Brittney was a bit put out by the increase in price, but she didn’t understand at that time why we only have 4 classes per week. It takes a full day to prep for each class. After our test finding, we decided on a Class of 9 at USD 70 per person. Read why Brittney is now one of our biggest supporters and why she now feels our cooking school is an amazing value!
