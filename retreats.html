Tresna Bali Culinary and Culture Retreats (CulCul Retreats)

CulCul is a play on words. “KulKul” is the traditional Balinese communication system in every village centre. When it rings, everyone knows immediately what it means. There are different sounds for different needs - one rhythm to announce the beginning of a ceremony, another urgent banging to get your bucket to help with a fire! As a Balinese, you grow up knowing exactly what each sound is in your village. However, you’ll be lost if you go into the next village as the sounds are different.

Tourists visiting Bali often have a hard time learning about the “real Bali”. Although the Balinese are beyond friendly and caring, due to language and cultural differences, it is very challenging for them to explain their way of life to tourists. You’re not on the same “KulKul” system.

With our CulCul Retreats, you’ll have your very own “KulKul”. With me, you’ll gain an inside perspective of Bali’s sacred culture and food. I’ve lived here for 19 years.
