Providing you with authentic Balinese experiences.

Frances and Adi have cooked for hundreds of foodies from all over the world, eaten in thousands of roadside warungs as well as created the most decadent wedding feasts with the top Chefs in Bali before building Tresna Bali Cooking School from ground up.

Frances became Bali’s only Holyperson from outside of Bali through a remarkable 6-year process from 2010-2016. For the first time, she’ll share her personal journey with you - from when she first set foot in this Paradise on October 7, 2000.

19 years later, she and Adi manage 5 enterprising small businesses all revolving around their passions of food, culture, healing and spirituality.

Behind Bali's Walls

Without a doubt, Bali is beautiful and the Balinese are a loving and peaceful society. However, the majority of Balinese locals have difficulty describing the rich culture behind the walls of their family compounds.

You’ll experience a Bali that is rarely shared with visitors as you learn about Adi and Frances’ devotion to Balinese Temple life and their passionate love affair with the best food on the island,

Even if you have been to Bali 20 times before, with this unique retreat, you’ll experience authentic local flavours, traverse the natural landscape, and embrace what it means to be truly Balinese.

Your Accommodation

Located in the charming village of Umalas, near Seminyak, Villa Malaathina – set among 5,000 square metres of immaculate tropical gardens and surrounded by traditional Balinese rice paddies – is your luxurious home for the duration of our 6-day retreat.

As an upmarket travel agent and wedding planner for 15 years in Bali, Frances is intimately familiar with every 5-star resort and villa in Bali. For own retreat, she has selected the very best villa for a group retreat - where you enjoy privacy in your luxurious room as well as world class facilities such as a 25-metre pool, dedicated yoga studio, media room and several indoor and outdoor spaces to relax. We have a team of Balinese and Canadian cooks, housekeepers and travel coordinators to take care of your every need.
