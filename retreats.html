Looking for a gastronomical retreat where you are immersed in the mysticism of Balinese culture?

Our culinary and cultural retreat is reserved for only 12 participants to bask in a luxurious villa where your every need is taken care of by our resident experts.

You’ll gain an inside perspective of Bali’s sacred culture through magical experiences, curated personally by our Resident Balinese Holyperson (Priestess) and Wedding Organizer, Frances Tse Ardika

Frances has eaten in thousands of roadside warungs as well as created the most sumptious wedding feasts with the top Chefs in Bali over 19 years before building an authentic cooking school from ground up. She has spent a lot of time praying in Temples across the island, as she became Bali’s only Holyperson from outside of Bali.

For the first time, she’ll share her personal journey with you from when she first set foot in this Paradise on October 7, 2000. 19 years later, she owns 5 enterprising small businesses all revolving around her passions of food, culture, healing and spirituality.

Providing you with authentic Balinese experiences.

Without a doubt, Bali is beautiful. However, most people who visit the island miss an opportunity to discover real Balinese Culture. With Frances’ deep insights and real Balinese life, you’ll experience a Bali that is rarely shared with visitors.

Even if you have been to Bali 20 times before, with this unique retreat, you’ll experience authentic local flavours, traverse the natural landscape, and embrace what it means to be truly Balinese.

Your Accommodation

Located in the charming village of Umalas, near Seminyak, Villa Malaathina – set among 5,000 square metres of immaculate tropical gardens and surrounded by traditional Balinese rice paddies – is your luxurious home for the duration of our 6-day retreat.

As an upmarket travel agent and wedding planner for 15 years in Bali, Frances is intimately familiar with every 5-star resort and villa in Bali. For own retreat, she has selected the very best villa for a group retreat - where you enjoy privacy in your luxurious room as well as world class facilities such as a 25-metre pool, dedicated yoga studio, media room and several indoor and outdoor spaces to relax. We have a team of Balinese and Canadian cooks, housekeepers and travel coordinators to take care of your every need.
